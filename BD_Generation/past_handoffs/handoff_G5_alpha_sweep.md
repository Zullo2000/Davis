## Key findings (Round 3)

1. **α=0.01 is the new optimal for no-remasking** — 69.0% satisfaction (was 53.5% at α=0.1). The harder constraint set shifted optimal α by 10×.
2. **Confidence remasking is α-insensitive** at 0.01–0.15 (flat ~55–57%), then drops. Remasking is the dominant effect.
3. **No-remasking beats confidence at K=16** — 69.0% vs 57.3% best.
4. **Remasking fights guidance at 98/100 steps** for ALL α values (negative remasking delta confirmed across the board).
5. **Quality cost at α=0.01**: diversity -4pp, mode coverage -28pp, cond. edge TV +0.15. Pareto knee around α=0.05.
